Project Overview

Designed and tested an analog audio equalizer with independent bass, midrange, and treble control using active filtering and amplification.

Tools Used

  • LTSpice

What I Did

  • Designed analog filters for bass, midrange, and treble bands
  • Simulated frequency response and verified with lab measurements
  • Debugged gain and stability issues in op-amp stages

What I Learned

  • Tradeoffs between active vs passive filtering
  • How component tolerances affect frequency response
  • Practical op-amp limitations beyond ideal models
