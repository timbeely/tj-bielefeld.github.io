Overview

Authored a research paper analyzing Artificial Intelligence’s impact on engineering practices both ethically and developmentally for Advanced Systems Engineering Corporation, a systems engineering and consulting company.

Technical Stack

  • Academic research and technical writing
  • Literature review of AI applications in engineering and business
  • Ethical analysis of machine learning systems
  • Systems engineering perspective on AI integration

Engineering Approach

  • Researched real-world AI implementations in engineering design, project management, and business operations.
  • Analyzed benefits such as automation, optimization, and predictive decision-making alongside ethical risks.
  • Evaluated case studies highlighting algorithmic bias, data integrity issues, and security vulnerabilities.

Key Takeaways

  • AI can significantly improve efficiency in engineering and business when used as a decision-support tool.
  • Biased or poorly curated training data can lead to critical ethical and operational failures.
  • Responsible AI implementation requires human oversight, transparency, and secure system design.
